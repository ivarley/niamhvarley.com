The Hedge Labyrinth

a Goose Chase, for Niamh aged ten

Today you turn ten. I have spent the better part of the year making a list of all the things I love most about you — and rather than scribbling them on a card, I have tied each one to a balloon and let them loose in my labyrinth.

There are ten balloons. (Of course there are ten. You are ten.) Find every one. Read what I have written. They are all true.

If you have a keyboard: walk forward with the up arrow, back with down, and turn with left and right. If you are on an iPad or a phone: four buttons will appear at the bottom of the screen — ↑ to walk, ↓ to back up, ↺ ↻ to turn. Tap and hold to keep moving.

And once every balloon is yours, come find me at the heart of the maze.

Honk softly, The Goose
